Making a drone was the first hardware electronics project i ever made. I ordered all the parts from online and that was a hard part as some parts were not sold at most places. Because the drone required soldering i had to teach myself which took a bit of time as i had never done it before and i often struggled to get the soldering iron to melt the solder.  After the drone was done and i went to fly it, i realized the propellers i had put on it weren't big enough to fly the drone so i had to order new ones. In the end it was a fun project and taught me how to solder, use mission planner and also figured out how to make the drone fly on it's on with mission planner which was cool, except at the start as when it was finished flying it's route, it would just rise into the air instead of landing.
